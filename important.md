## Golden rules

Always apply to 2–3 jobs per day
Never rely on one application. Keep the pipeline full. While waiting for Company A, apply to B, C, D.

Speed matters
Apply within 24 hours of a job posting. Early applicants get reviewed first. Jobs with under 50 applicants are gold.

One message = maximum impact
Lead with Quranly + 1M+ downloads in every message. That number stops people from scrolling.

Referral beats resume
An internal referral from any employee — even from a different team — moves you from 500 applicants to top 5.
