### Emergency Interview Phrases (Memorize!)

**When nervous:**

- Take a deep breath
- Say: "Thank you for the question. Let me think for a moment."
- Smile and maintain eye contact

**When you don't understand:**

- "Could you please repeat that?"
- "I'm not sure I understand. Could you clarify?"

**When you don't know the answer:**

- "That's a great question. I haven't encountered that specific situation, but here's how I would approach it..."
- "I'm not familiar with that, but I'm a quick learner and would love to learn more about it."

**When you make a mistake:**

- Don't panic!
- Say: "Sorry, let me rephrase that..."
- Continue confidently

### Daily Checklist (Print & Use!)

**Morning:**

- [ ] Watch & analyze 1 scene (20 min)
- [ ] Learn 5-7 new words (15 min)
- [ ] Shadow speaking (10 min)

**Evening:**

- [ ] Record speaking practice (15 min)
- [ ] Job practice (20 min)
- [ ] Review yesterday's notes (10 min)

**Before Sleep:**

- [ ] Listen to English (10 min)
- [ ] Think about tomorrow in English (5 min)

### Most Important Rules

1. **NEVER skip a day** - even 15 minutes is better than nothing
2. **ALWAYS record yourself** - listening back is crucial
3. **SPEAK OUT LOUD** - thinking isn't enough
4. **USE NEW WORDS** - don't just collect, use them!
5. **THINK IN ENGLISH** - stop translating from Urdu
6. **BE PATIENT** - language learning takes time
7. **CELEBRATE PROGRESS** - notice how far you've come
8. **STAY CONSISTENT** - this is the secret to success
