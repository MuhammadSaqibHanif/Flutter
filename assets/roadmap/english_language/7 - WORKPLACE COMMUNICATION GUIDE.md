### Daily Office Phrases

**Morning Greetings**:

- "Good morning, everyone!"
- "How's it going?"
- "How was your weekend?"
- "Did you have a good evening?"

**Starting Work**:

- "Let me check my emails first"
- "I'll start working on [task]"
- "I need to finish [task] today"
- "I have a meeting at [time]"

**Asking for Help**:

- "Could you please help me with something?"
- "I'm stuck on [problem]. Do you have a moment?"
- "Would you mind reviewing my code?"
- "Can you explain how [system] works?"
- "I need your input on something"

**Offering Help**:

- "Do you need any help?"
- "I can take a look at that if you want"
- "Let me know if you need anything"
- "I'm happy to help with [task]"

**In Meetings**:

- "Thanks for joining"
- "Let's get started"
- "Does everyone understand?"
- "Any questions so far?"
- "Let me share my screen"
- "Can you hear me?"
- "Sorry, I was on mute"

**Giving Updates**:

- "Quick update: I finished [task]"
- "I'm currently working on [task]"
- "I'll have it ready by [time/date]"
- "It's taking longer than expected because..."
- "We're on track to finish on time"

**Reporting Problems**:

- "We have a problem with [system]"
- "I found a bug in [feature]"
- "The server is down"
- "This is taking longer than planned"
- "We need to discuss [issue]"

**Making Suggestions**:

- "What if we try [solution]?"
- "I suggest we [action]"
- "Have you considered [option]?"
- "Maybe we could [idea]"
- "I think we should [recommendation]"

**Agreeing/Disagreeing**:

- "I agree with you"
- "That makes sense"
- "Good point"
- "I see what you mean, but..."
- "I have a different perspective"
- "I'm not sure about that because..."

**Ending Conversations**:

- "Alright, let's do that"
- "Sounds good"
- "I'll get started on this"
- "Let me know if anything changes"
- "Talk to you later"
- "Have a great day!"

### Email Phrases Converted to Speaking

**Email**: "Please find attached"  
**Speaking**: "I've attached the file" or "I'm sending you the file"

**Email**: "As per our discussion"  
**Speaking**: "As we discussed" or "Like we talked about"

**Email**: "I would appreciate if you could"  
**Speaking**: "Could you please" or "Can you"

**Email**: "Please do not hesitate to contact me"  
**Speaking**: "Let me know if you need anything" or "Feel free to reach out"

**Email**: "I am writing to inform you"  
**Speaking**: "I wanted to let you know" or "Just to inform you"

### Meeting Types & Key Phrases

**Standup Meeting** (Daily team update):

- "Yesterday I worked on [task]"
- "Today I'll work on [task]"
- "I'm blocked by [problem]"
- "I need help with [issue]"
- "Everything is going well"

**Planning Meeting** (Discussing upcoming work):

- "Let's plan the next sprint"
- "What are our priorities?"
- "How long will this take?"
- "Who will work on this?"
- "Let's break this into smaller tasks"

**Review Meeting** (Showing completed work):

- "Let me show you what we've built"
- "This feature works like this..."
- "We completed [items]"
- "We faced some challenges with [issue]"
- "Here's what we'll do next"

**Retrospective** (Discussing what went well/wrong):

- "What went well this sprint?"
- "What could we improve?"
- "I think we should continue [practice]"
- "Next time, let's try [suggestion]"

**Code Review**:

- "I reviewed your code"
- "The logic looks good"
- "I have a few suggestions"
- "Could you explain this part?"
- "This might cause issues because..."
- "Good job on [aspect]"

### Presentation Phrases

**Opening**:

- "Thank you for your time today"
- "Today I'll talk about [topic]"
- "I'll cover three main points"
- "First, let me give you some background"

**During Presentation**:

- "Let me show you..."
- "As you can see here..."
- "This diagram shows..."
- "For example..."
- "Moving on to the next point..."

**Handling Questions**:

- "That's a great question"
- "Let me address that"
- "I'll come back to that in a moment"
- "Good point, let me clarify"

**Closing**:

- "To summarize..."
- "In conclusion..."
- "The key takeaways are..."
- "Thank you for listening"
- "Any questions?"
