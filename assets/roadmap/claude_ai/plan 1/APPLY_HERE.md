## Part 1 — Applying When No Job Is Posted

This is called a **cold application** or **speculative application**. It works better than you think for strong candidates.

---

### The 3-Step Cold Application Method

**Step 1 — Find the right person (not HR)**

Don't email generic HR. Find:
- Engineering Manager
- CTO or VP Engineering
- Mobile Team Lead
- Director of Product

Go to LinkedIn → search `"Engineering Manager" "Company Name"` → find a real person.

---

**Step 2 — Send this exact cold email**

Find their email using:
- **Hunter.io** (free tier) — type company domain, it finds emails
- **Apollo.io** (free tier) — same
- **LinkedIn message** if email not found

Email template:
```
Subject: Senior Flutter Developer — 2M+ Downloads App, Open to Joining [Company]

Hi [Name],

I'm a Senior Flutter Developer with 7 years of experience, 
currently lead engineer on Quranly — a cross-platform app 
with 2M+ downloads, 4.8★ rating, and 0.10% crash rate.

I notice [Company] builds [what they build]. I'd love to 
bring this same level of ownership to your mobile team.

I'm not applying to a specific posting — I'm reaching out 
because I genuinely respect what your team has built and 
believe I can add immediate value.

Would you be open to a 15-minute call?

CV: [link to your resume]
App: play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.quranly.app

Best,
Muhammad Saqib Hanif
+92 333-3471969
```

**Step 3 — Follow up once after 5 days**

```
Subject: Following up — Flutter Developer Inquiry

Hi [Name], just following up on my message from last week. 
Happy to share more about my work on Quranly if helpful.
```
