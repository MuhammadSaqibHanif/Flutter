## 💰 SALARY NEGOTIATION SCRIPT

**When they ask: "What's your expected salary?"**

**DON'T SAY:**
❌ "PKR 1,000,000" (too specific, locks you in)
❌ "I'm flexible" (looks desperate)
❌ "Whatever you think is fair" (leaves money on table)

**DO SAY:**
✅ "Based on my research for Senior Flutter Developers with 6+ years experience and a track record of scaling apps to 1M+ users, I'm looking in the range of $80K-$100K annually, but I'm open to discussing the complete package including benefits and growth opportunities. What's the budget for this role?"

**If they push back:**
"I currently earn PKR 350,000/month (≈$42K/year). Given my experience scaling applications to 1M+ users and leading teams, plus the current market rates for senior Flutter developers, I believe $80K-$100K is fair. However, I'm interested in the total package and growth potential. What can you offer?"

**When you get an offer:**

1. **Thank them:**
   "Thank you so much! I'm excited about this opportunity."

2. **Ask for time:**
   "Can I have 2-3 days to review the details?"

3. **Negotiate:**
   "I'm very interested in joining [Company]. The offer is at $75K, and I was hoping we could discuss $85K-$90K based on my experience. Is there flexibility?"

4. **Use competing offers:**
   "I have another offer at $85K, but I'm more excited about [Company] because of [reason]. Can you match or come close?"

5. **Don't accept immediately:**
   Even if it's great, say: "This looks good. Let me review everything and get back to you tomorrow."
