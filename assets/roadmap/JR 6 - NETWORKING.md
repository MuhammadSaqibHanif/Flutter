## Referral Strategy

### Building Silicon Valley Network from Pakistan

**LinkedIn Strategy:**

**Month 1-2: Profile Optimization & Warm-Up**

1. Optimize profile (covered above)
2. Post consistently (2-3x/week)
3. Engage with Flutter content
4. Follow target company employees

**Month 3-4: Strategic Connections**

1. **Identify Targets:**

   - Flutter developers at target companies
   - Engineering managers
   - Recruiters (search "[Company] Technical Recruiter")
   - Fellow Pakistanis at these companies

2. **Connection Request Template:**

```

Hi [Name],

I came across your profile and noticed you're working on [specific project/team]
at [Company]. I'm particularly interested in [relevant topic from their posts/profile].

I'm a Flutter developer with 6 years of mobile development experience, and I'm
exploring opportunities in [location]. Would love to connect and learn more about
your experience at [Company].

Best regards,
[Your Name]

```

**Do:**

- Be specific about what caught your attention
- Keep it brief
- Be genuine
- Mention something from their profile/posts

**Don't:**

- Ask for referral in first message
- Send generic templates
- Be overly salesy

**Month 5-6: Relationship Building**

1. **Engage with Their Content:**

   - Like and thoughtfully comment on posts
   - Share relevant insights
   - Don't just say "Great post!"

2. **Provide Value:**

   - Share interesting Flutter resources
   - Offer to help with their projects
   - Write about topics they're interested in

3. **Warm Intro Request (after relationship)**

```

Hi [Name],

Hope you're doing well! I've really enjoyed our conversations about [topic].

I'm actively looking for Flutter opportunities and noticed [Company] has an
opening for [role]. Given your experience there, I was wondering if you'd be
comfortable providing a referral or intro to the hiring manager?

I've attached my resume and would be happy to share more about my background
and projects.

Totally understand if timing isn't right - either way, thanks for all the
insights you've shared!

Best,
[Your Name]

```

**Referral Process:**

- Most companies pay $1K-$5K referral bonus
- This incentivizes employees to refer
- Your job is to make it easy for them

**Make It Easy:**

1. Share polished resume
2. Include portfolio link
3. Have 1-paragraph summary of your background
4. Explain why you're interested in their company
5. Highlight relevant experience

## Recruiter Engagement

### When to Work with Recruiters

**Good for:**

- Access to unadvertised roles
- Salary negotiation support
- Interview preparation insights
- Multiple opportunities simultaneously

**Be Cautious:**

- Not all recruiters understand Flutter/mobile
- May push wrong opportunities
- Commission-driven

### Finding Good Recruiters

**Where:**

- LinkedIn (search "Mobile Recruiter [Company]")
- AngelList (startup recruiters)
- Hired.com, Vettery, Terminal

**Red Flags:**

- Mass messages with no personalization
- Don't understand Flutter
- Push roles that don't match
- Unprofessional communication
